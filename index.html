What is Nextech? Nextech is a program that focuses on a specific goal to increase the number of Indiana students that choose to pursue a career in technology upon high school graduation, in order to accomlish this goal they take student from high school and train them. The Nextech program focus on coding and making websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
